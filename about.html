About

Hi I am su, a UX designer from Bangalore, India. I have a background in graphic design and started my career by dabbling in different kinds of design. When I tried UX design as an undergrad design intern, I absolutely fell in love with it. Specializing in end-to-end UX, I drive decisions with data and user insights, ensuring products align with both user needs and strategic objectives. My multidisciplinary background enables a holistic approach, pushing design boundaries to create meaningful, accessible, and scalable experiences.

Design Philosophy

Team Collaboration is everything

Driven by structured processes

Better communication = Better design

I believe that one of the most important jobs of a designer is to ensure smooth communication of ideas amongst different teams while building a product. Designing and facilitating design workshops is something that I really enjoy. Apart from that I also have an intense fascination towards learning and creating new design processes.
